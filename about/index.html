About

My name is Sean Quigley and I am a scientist with interests in genetic and molecular engineering, synthetic biology, and data science.Hailing from the Garden State, I went to school in Vermont, and am currently living in the Streeterville neighborhood of Chicago. Currently, I am a research scientist at Evozyne, a molecular engineering startup that sprouted from the University of Chicago. At Evozyne, I do a mixture of wet lab work and bioinformatic analysis.

When not in the lab, I fill my time with a variety of activities. Currently, my curiosity has been piqued by 3D printing/CAD; in addition, I enjoy all things related to the outdoors, a quality documentary, and making highly curated playlists. I love learning new things and tend to get overexcited about random knowledge that will only prove to be useful when watching jeopardy.

I chose to make this website so that I could have a place to document various projects, thoughts, or really whatever I want in a format that I find aesthetically pleasing. I originally wanted to make the domain name some kind of pun centered around the idea that making a website in one’s own name is vain and egotistic, but I wasn’t clever enough to come up with one I thought was worthwhile. C'est la vie. I swear, I’m not that conceited.
